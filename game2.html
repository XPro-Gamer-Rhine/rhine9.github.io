DAWN OF MAN

Dawn Of Man is a post apocalyptic game. In the future the world almost ended by human inventions. A rift in space time was created due to the malfunction of the large hadron collider. The center of the world hosted the wormhole. A group of scientist and solders join together to hurness the power the wormhole to create a time machine to correct the past. But the travel was unexpected and the main character ended up in different historical of time. Now he must Alter slightly to change the future. But if he alters too much then reality might change for the worse. You now play as this character.
Find your equipments & complete your mission.
